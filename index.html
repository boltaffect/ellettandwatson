The office of Dr. Ellett and Dr. Watson is centered on maintaining good oral health, but also on building and maintaining personal relationships with each and every patient.

If you know Dr. Watson, you know he never meets a stranger and whether you are related to him or not, you will feel like family by the time you leave the office. By adding Dr. Ellett to the practice, Dr. Watson has assured that this tradition will continue for years to come.

From the time you walk in the door, until the time you leave, we want to make sure that you are treated like a part of our family. Whether you are young or old, a new patient or a lifelong regular, we want to make you feel at home.

Please feel free to call or email us if you would like to schedule an appointment.
