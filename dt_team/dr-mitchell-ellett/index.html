Dr. Mitchell Ellett

Dr. Ellett is originally from Tupelo, MS, but has been in Greenville since 2008. He graduated from Millsaps College in Jackson, MS in 1998 with a B.S. in biology and got his dental degree from the Medical College of Georgia School of Dentistry in Augusta, GA in 2008. He and his wife Paige have been married since 2001 and have two children, Eliza and Thomas.

When not practicing dentistry, Dr. Ellett enjoys fishing, working in the yard, spending time with his family and Ole Miss football. He is an active member of Grace Church, a member of the American Dental Association, South Carolina Dental Association, Greenville County Dental Society and volunteers regularly with the Greenville Free Medical and Dental Clinic.
